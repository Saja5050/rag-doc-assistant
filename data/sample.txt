Travel Guide - Sample Document

- Paris is known as the city of lights and has famous landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.
- Tokyo is one of the busiest cities in the world, with a blend of modern skyscrapers and traditional temples.
- New York City is famous for Times Square, Central Park, and its diverse culture.
- Rome is rich in history, with attractions like the Colosseum and the Roman Forum.
- London is home to the Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the River Thames.
